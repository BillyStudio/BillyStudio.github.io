Cross correlation in Machine learning

Feb 20 2019 2 minutes read (About 264 words)

The discrict 2D cross correlation is very similar to 2D convolution.

check by MATLAB code

M1 = [17 24  1  8 15;
      23  5  7 14 16;
       4  6 13 20 22;
      10 12 19 21  3;
      11 18 25  2  9];
M2 = [8 1 6;
      3 5 7;
      4 9 2];
D = xcorr2(M1, M2);
D
